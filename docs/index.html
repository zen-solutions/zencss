    Getting Started

    Begin by following the setup instructions here to enable zenCSS on your site.     Desktop-First Design Philosophy

    Embrace a desktop-centric workflow. Craft your website's content for desktop interfaces, and trust zenCSS to adeptly translate the design for tablet and mobile presentations, ensuring a harmonious user experience across all device landscapes.

    Quick Overview - The zenCSS Grid System

    Containers - Containers are distinct sections of your webpage such as a header or hero section. Each container is a resolution-friendly box, adept at managing horizontal alignment across mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.

    Rows - Rows provide the scaffolding for a section, and are stacked like cake layers. Add as many or few as you need to a container — we've baked in smart flex logic to ensure they work beautifully. Rows house any number of columns, which in turn contain a collection of HTML elements.

    Columns - Whether it's a section heading, a paragraph paired with an image, or even a themed design based on the fibonaci sequence, columns are up for the task. They auto-adjust for siblings as well as tablet and mobile views. They can also "be" things, such as a card. Columns align perfectly at all resolutions — even in separate rows.

    Kickstarting with a Basic Example

    Let's begin with a straightforward example of our signature grid layout for a site section using semantic HTML: 

    <main class="z-container">
<section class="z-row">
<div class="z-col"> ... </div>
</section>
</main>

    The flexibility of zenCSS shines through as the number of columns per row (typically 2-6) or HTML items per column is irrelevant—zenCSS manages the layout gracefully. Enrich a content area by adding more rows or columns. It’s that simple.

    If brevity appeals to you, meet Zentax, our shorthand HTML syntax that halves your coding effort: 

    <z-container>
<z-row>
<z-col> ... </z-col>
</z-row>
</z-container>

    zenCSS Text System - Automatic, dynamic, responsive resizing

    zenCSS ships with a dynamic text scaling feature, auto-adjusting font sizes across varying viewport resolutions to ensure readability and aesthetic consistency.

    Out of the box, our standard text sizes are:

    • 14px on mobile
    • 16 px on tablet
    • 18 on desktop screens

    The heading tags (h1 - h6) are smartly configured to resize at each breakpoint, based on the default font size. For a deeper dive, inspect an element below and switch to the computed tab in your browser's developer tools while toggling the responsive view. As you resize the window, you'll observe the font sizes adapting to the changing viewport dimensions.
