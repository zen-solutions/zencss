Cookie consent

A tool for obtaining user consent for cookies, helping support compliance with privacy laws.

Consent form will display at the bottom of the page. Upon acceptance, a local storage object is created and the value is saved for them automatically.

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. By continuing to use our site, you agree to our use of cookies. Learn more in our Privacy & Cookie Policy

Code Example Shortcut: z-cookie-consent 
<div class="cookie-consent-banner">
    <p> Our website uses cookies to ... Learn more in our 
      <a href="#">Privacy & Cookie Policy</a>
    </p>
    <button class="accept-cookies btn-md">Accept</button>
</div>
