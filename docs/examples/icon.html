Icon Containers

Wrap your icons in round, square or rounded containers. Adjust size and color to your liking.

Code Example 
<div class="icon-circle bg-primary w-30 h-30">
    <img src="icon.svg" class="icon-invert" alt="">
</div>

<div class="icon-circle round bg-primary w-40 h-40">
    <img src="icon.svg" class="icon-invert" alt="">
</div>

<div class="icon-circle square bg-primary">
    <img src="icon.svg" class="icon-invert" alt="">
</div>
