Padding & Margin Utilities Documentation

General Padding and Margin:

The size increases by 0.25rem for each level starting from 0.

  • .p-0 to .p-5: Applies padding around an element.
  • .m-0 to .m-5: Applies margin around an element.

Specific Side Padding and Margin:

The size increases by 0.75em for each level starting from 0.

  • .mt-0 to .mt-5: Applies top margin to an element.
  • .mb-0 to .mb-5: Applies bottom margin to an element.
  • .ml-0 to .ml-5: Applies left margin to an element.
  • .mr-0 to .mr-5: Applies right margin to an element.
  • .pt-0 to .pt-5: Applies top padding to an element.
  • .pb-0 to .pb-5: Applies bottom padding to an element.
  • .pl-0 to .pl-5: Applies left padding to an element.
  • .pr-0 to .pr-5: Applies right padding to an element.

Extended Padding and Margin Classes:

  • .p-6: Applies 2em padding around an element.
  • .m-6: Applies 2em margin around an element.
  • .p-7: Applies 3em padding around an element.
  • .m-7: Applies 3vw margin around an element.
