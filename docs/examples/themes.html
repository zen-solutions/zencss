Site Themes & Modes

zenCSS offers both light & dark mode with 7 dynamic themes.

We offer a default, light and dark theme, plus 4 individual themes based on your brand colors, allowing you to style individual content items and their children based on your signature style. These seven meticulously developed themes aim to offer instant color and styling flexibility while maintaining WCAG compliance. Our system provides support to override background and text colors on any theme element using our text and background utility classes.
