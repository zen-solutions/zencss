Site Themes & Modes

zenCSS offers both light & dark mode with 7 dynamic themes.

We offer a default light and dark (beta) theme, plus 6 individual component themes which override light & dark mode, allowing you to style individual content items and their children based on your brand colors and design preferences. These seven meticulously developed themes aim to offer instant color and styling flexibility while maintaining WCAG compliance. Or simply override background and text colors on any theme element using our text and background utility classes.
