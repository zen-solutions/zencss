Site Themes & Modes

zenCSS ships with light & dark mode plus 7 dynamic, accessable themes.

 WCAG 2.1 AA

We offer a default, light and dark theme, plus 4 individual themes based on your brand colors, allowing you to style individual content items and their children based on your signature style. These seven meticulously developed themes offer instant color and styling flexibility while maintaining WCAG 2.1 AA compliance. Our system lets you easily override background and text colors on any theme element using our Smart-Color System.
