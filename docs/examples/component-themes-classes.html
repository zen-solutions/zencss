Component Themes Classes

Component Themes Classes:

.theme-primary: Stylizes your content with a primary color theme.

.theme-dark: Provides a dark themed styling for your content.

.theme-secondary: Applies a secondary color theme to your content.

.theme-accent: Stylizes your content with an accent color theme.

.theme-cta: Provides a Call To Action (CTA) color theme for your content.

Additionally, the following classes provide an accent line on the left side of the element with a color respective to the theme:

.theme-dark.accent-left

.theme-secondary.accent-left

.theme-cta.accent-left

.theme-light.accent-left
